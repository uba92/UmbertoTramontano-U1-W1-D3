Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia apetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350 g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

    • For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brusching on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife is desired, though is not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia doug

    In a medium-sizing mixing bowl, or in a stan mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the doug ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the doug, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
